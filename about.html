Hello Everyone, Welcome!

Thank you so much for taking the time to visit my website about my travels. If theres one thing I can talk about it would have to be my endeavours all around the world. There are so many places to see in so little time so I tell all my friends to take the risk and book the flight!

I grew up traveling the world thanks to my amazing mother who was a Delta flight attendent for 23 years. Given that employees get travel benefits which also include the entire family of that employee, I was able to fly for free which I am blessed and thankful for. I try to share tips and tricks and places I recommend. Sit back, relax, and read my thoughts on where and why you should travel to the places I've been!
