The Best Cinnamon Rolls

Here's how to make the most delicious cinnamon rolls on the planet. By the time you master this recipe, you will never go back to eating cinnamon rolls made from other bakers.


ingredients recipe

Ingredients

  • All purpose flower
  • Powdered sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Yeast
  • Baking powder
  • Butter
  • 2 eggs

Recipe

  1. Set oven to 350F
  2. Mix 2 eggs with melted butter until smooth.
  3. Slowly add warm milk while mixing butter and eggs.
  4. Mix flour, salt, powdered sugar, and yeast.
  5. Add yeast to wet ingredients.
  6. Slowly add flour to wet ingredients.
  7. Knead dough on countertop until perfect consistency.
  8. Place dough in a bowl and cover the bowl with clear wrap, place in the oven while off and let sit for 2 hours so that the dough can rise.
  9. Roll dough out and cut even cinnamon rolls.
  10. Place in a buttered pan, bake for 14 minutes.
