Where to find the best damn cookies

Where should you go?

1. Phenomenom

Six ounces of hearty, delicious, warm cookie. Go for a classic chocolate chip or if you have an extra sweet tooth, dive into a M&M oreo cookie which will nourish your hunger. Open late at night.

2. Fireman Dereks

Located in the heart of Wynwood, this tiny bakeshop offers some of the best cookies miami has to offer. If you enjoy a perfect sized cookie that is induced with just the right amount of sweetness hot out of the oven, fireman Dereks is your place.

3. Night Owl Cookies

Hello Fresh! If you are looking for a perfect snack, look no more! Texture is on point and a wide array of flavors means you never get bored. You can never go wrong with the Cinnamon Toast Crunch, a dirty Diana or the cookies and creme!!! Indulge in pure yumminess! =

4. Stubborn Seed


Chef driven american restaurant that offers fine dining, but also the best snickerdoodle cookies Miami has to offer. Indulge in a warm, thick cookie with a glass of milk. This will not leave you hungry.
