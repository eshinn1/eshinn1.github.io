My Travel Skills

Expert Flight Research

Ever since I was 15 years old (I'm 22 now), friends and family would always reach out to me to assist them in booking their airfare. The secret is using Google Flights. Google Flights allows you to input more than 1 city for both departure and arrival airport. They also let you search via a calendar view which shows you the prices for everyday of the month! When in doubt, always use Google Flights to book your air travel!

Proper Timing

It is important to always remember when the most expensive time to travel is. That is usually around the holidyas, summer months, and when the kids are in school. My favorite time to travel is between September and early November or late March to lay May.

Free Flights and Hotel Stays

One of the best ways to travel for free is by utlizing credit card sign up bonuses. My best redemption was when I flew business class to Japan on All Nippon Airlines for free. Thanks to my American Express sign-on bonus, I was able to wine and dine at 38,000 feet without paying a dime!

Who doesn't love suprises!?

My heart feels the most warm when I take my friends or family to places I have been that I know they will love. I took my mom to Cappadoccia, Turkey in the fall of 2020 as Turkey was one of the first countries to open up after the Pandemic for American tourists. I told my mom the night before that I planned a fun excursion but she had no clue what she was in for! I ended up taking her up in a private hot air balloon which guided us all around the mountains of northern Turkey.

Don't Plan Activities Beforehand

Most people go by the rule of planning daily activties before your trip starts but I prefer to just book the flight and plan when you get there. This allows you to ask locals in person what THEY thnk you should do, not travel bloggers on Google who most likely don't have familiarity with the area.

Have Fun

It is important to enjoy yourself whereever the world may take you. Don't let the stress of missing a train or bus ruin your entire trip. Take a deep breath and remember that everything happens for a reason!
